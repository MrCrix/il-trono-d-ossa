# Prompt per NanoBanana2 - Il Trono d'Ossa

Prompt ottimizzati per generazione immagini dei personaggi principali della campagna.

## 1. Lord Orsinar Tharavos

### Prompt Completo

```
A distinguished 60-year-old nobleman and antiquities merchant, portrait painting style. Robust build, salt-and-pepper hair pulled back in a neat ponytail secured with an elegant silver sun-shaped clasp. Piercing green eyes that study everything carefully behind silver-framed spectacles. Wearing luxurious black velvet robes embroidered with intricate golden sun motifs. White silk cloth in hand for cleaning glasses. Signet ring with golden sun on black field visible on finger. Small arc-shaped scar above right eyebrow. Warm, intelligent expression radiating wisdom and absolute honesty. Background: shelves with ancient artifacts, glass display cases, warm candlelight. D&D fantasy style, oil painting quality, dignified scholarly atmosphere, Lathander worshipper aesthetic, mature distinguished appearance, professional quality.
```

### Caratteristiche Chiave
- **Età**: 60 anni, robusto
- **Capelli**: Brizzolati, coda con fermaglio sole d'argento
- **Occhi**: Verdi penetranti
- **Abbigliamento**: Velluto nero con ricami solari dorati
- **Accessori**: Occhiali d'argento, panno di seta bianco
- **Atmosfera**: Studioso, autorevole, onesto

### Negative Prompt
```
young, evil, sinister, greedy merchant, fat, slovenly, dirty, poor clothing, modern, cyberpunk, anime, cartoon, muscular warrior, aggressive
```

## Istruzioni per NanoBanana2

### Parametri Consigliati
- **Stile**: Fantasy portrait, D&D character art, oil painting quality
- **Aspect Ratio**: 2:3 (portrait verticale) o 1:1 (quadrato)
- **Quality**: Alta/Massima
- **Steps**: 30-50
- **CFG Scale**: 7-8

### Note Importanti
1. **Orsinar**: Enfatizzare età (60 anni), saggezza, velluto nero con oro
2. **Velloran**: Enfatizzare ciocca bianca, taccuino nero, atmosfera misteriosa
3. **Valdric**: Enfatizzare armatura nera, tragicità, fiore bianco, non soldato moderno

### Ordine di Priorità
Se devi generare solo alcune immagini, priorità:
1. **Orsinar** (centrale nel Preludio)
2. **Valdric** (antagonista principale)
3. **Velloran** (secondario ma importante)

### Stile 4: Painted Masterwork

## Troubleshooting

**Se Orsinar esce troppo giovane:**
Aggiungi: `elderly nobleman, 60 years old, mature age, wrinkles, silver hair`

**Se Velloran esce senza vestiti:**
Aggiungi: `fully clothed, wearing elegant black robes, aristocratic clothing`

**Se Valdric esce come soldato moderno:**
Aggiungi: `medieval fantasy knight, full plate armor, fantasy RPG, NOT modern soldier, NOT WW1`

**Se i colori sono sbagliati:**
Specifica meglio: `black velvet with gold embroidery` / `pure black clothing` / `blackened steel armor`

**Se lo scrigno esce aperto o con effetti magici:**
Aggiungi: `firmly closed shut locked chest, no magic, no glow, no supernatural effects, mundane ancient artifact`

## 7. Scrigno Antico di Ralen Thorn (Item)

### Prompt Completo

```
An ancient closed wooden chest resting on a rough stone surface. Made of dark blackened oak aged 2000-3000 years, reinforced with heavily oxidized green-patina bronze fittings and corner brackets. The chest is 40cm wide, 25cm deep, 20cm tall, small and compact. The wood surface shows signs of prolonged burial: earth stains, mineral deposits, fine cracks from centuries underground. The front panel is carved with an inscription in angular yet flowing archaic script, partially worn.
```
